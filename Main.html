There are 2 steps to record your time:
1. Choose the time table.
2. Click the activity/subactivity button.
There are 3 steps to create your own activities.
1: Click the "Create Your Own Activities" on the right side.
2: Input the activity name and choose the color.
3: Save your own activity.
There are 3 steps to create your own sub-activities.
1: Click the "Create Your Own Activities" on the right side.
2: Then click the "Create Sub-Activities" on the left corner.
3: Choose the activity and input the sub-activity name.
3: Save your own sub-activity.
There are 4 steps to create your own sub-activities.
1: Click the "Create Your Own Activities" on the right side.
2: Then click the "Create Sub-Activities" on the left corner.
3: Choose the activity and input the sub-activity name.
4: Save your own sub-activity.
There is 1 step to find your previous record.
